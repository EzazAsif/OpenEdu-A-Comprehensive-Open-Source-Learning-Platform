About OpenEDU

A Collaborative Learning Platform

OpenEDU is an open-source e-learning platform designed to democratize education by allowing students and faculty to share their knowledge freely.

Community Driven

Our platform thrives on contributions from educators and learners worldwide. Anyone can upload, edit, and improve educational content.

Rich Content Library

Access thousands of notes, slides, and videos across various subjects. All content is peer-reviewed and rated by the community.

For Students & Faculty

Whether you're learning or teaching, OpenEDU provides tools to enhance your educational journey with collaborative features.

Open Source

Our platform is completely open-source, allowing anyone to contribute to its development and customize it for their needs.

Features

Powerful tools for collaborative learning

OpenEDU provides everything you need to share knowledge and learn effectively.

Easy Content Upload

Upload your notes, slides, or videos in just a few clicks. Supports multiple formats including PDF, PPT, MP4, and more.

Smart Organization

Content is automatically categorized and tagged for easy discovery. Search by subject, topic, or keywords.
